About Fremd MUN

William Fremd Model United Nations, or WFMUN, is a competitive high school MUN team located in the Chicago suburb area. Operating in Palatine, Illinois, WFMUN attends several high school conferences in the area, including CIMUN and MUNI. The club meets every Tuesday at 3:00 PM in Room 264 at FHS.

Notable accomplishments of the WFMUN team include: Best Small Delegation at SIMUN XII, Outstanding Delegation at CIMUN XI, Outstanding Small Delegation at LYMUN II, Best Large Delegation at MUNI XXI, and Outstanding Small Delegation at CSMUN VII

WFMUN is currently run by our Executive Committee members: Naman Agarwal, Prayag Bhakar, Kaveen Desai, Jackson Hirsch, Hannah Jaros, Emma Liu, Hancine Mok, Chris Zhu, and our sponsor, Ankeet Mantra.
